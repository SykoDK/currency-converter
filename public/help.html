Help & FAQ

Use the nav links to navigate the website.

To change the chart on the home page click on one of the live rates.

To convert currencies, go to the converter page and enter the currencies and amount you want to convert.

To change the chart scale use the buttons listed below the chart.

To see the specific points on the chart, hover over the blue points.

To see the latest exchange rates, go to the home page.
